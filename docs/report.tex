\section{Supplementary Analysis: Law Adoption and Homicide Rates Over Time}\label{sec:supp}
% ======================================================================

\par
To complement the Tableau visualizations, an event-study style chart
(Figure~\ref{fig:lawadoption}) was generated with AI to show how the average
firearm homicide rate evolves around the moment a specific law is adopted.
For each law, year~0 is the year of first adoption in a given state; the
chart shows the average across all states that adopted the law, from five
years before to ten years after adoption.

\begin{quote}
	\textbf{Prompt:} \textit{Generate a multi line event study showing the
		average firearm homicide rate from 5 years before to 10 years after the
		adoption of 3 popular firearm laws vs 3 evidence based firearm laws.

		I want you to use the following laws for the study:

		- Gun violence RO
		- Assault weapons ban
		- LCM Ban

		as popular laws, and as evidence based laws use:

		- permit to purchase
		- waiting period
		- universal BG check

		Also include some metrics to compare the average between these at the
		bottom and simplify the axis labels to only show once for all charts.}
\end{quote}
